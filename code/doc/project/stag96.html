AIPS++ Scientific and Technical Advisory Group

The meeting will be held in the Array Operation Center in Socorro (see directions if you have not visited before). On the evening of Sunday November 3, members of the Advisory Group are invited to a reception at the home of Brian and Marie Glendenning (601 Park Street, 505 835 1211, for directions, see map)

Purpose of STAG Meeting

The purpose of the Scientific and Technical Advisory Group is to advise the Project Manager on scientific and technical matters relevant to AIPS++. During the meeting, project members will make presentations on the various areas of development in AIPS++. The emphasis will be on current capabilities and those intended to be added in the medium term. We will be particularly interested in advice on how solve areas of technical difficulty, and on scientific priorities. Instead of a mailing of documents to the AG members, we have put together an extensive reading list of relevant documents that STAG members might wish to peruse. This list matches the agenda given below. The material is intended to provide a background for the presentations.
